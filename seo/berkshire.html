Fire Compliance Services Across Berkshire

DE Fire Compliance provides fire door inspections and compartmentation surveys to commercial buildings, housing associations, care homes, schools, and managed properties throughout Berkshire. Based nearby in the Thames Valley, we offer fast response times and competitive rates across the county.

Berkshire's mix of commercial centres, large housing stock, and healthcare facilities creates significant demand for fire compliance services. From office blocks in Reading's town centre to care homes in rural West Berkshire, every building with fire doors needs regular inspection to meet current legislation.

Our inspectors are qualified carpenters and firestoppers who've progressed into surveying and inspecting — they understand construction as well as compliance. All reports are produced by an FDIS-qualified professional, meeting the requirements of BS 8214:2016, the Fire Safety Act 2021, and the Building Safety Act 2022. You receive clear, audit-ready documentation with prioritised remedial recommendations.
