Fire Compliance Services Across Greater London

We provide fire door inspections, compartmentation surveys, and compliance support to commercial buildings, housing associations, care homes, NHS facilities, and managed properties throughout London. Our inspectors are qualified carpenters and firestoppers who've progressed into surveying and inspecting. All reports are produced by an FDIS-qualified professional.

Under the Fire Safety Act 2021 and Building Safety Act 2022, the responsible person for any non-domestic premises must ensure fire doors are regularly inspected and compartmentation is properly maintained. Our surveys assess every element against BS 8214:2016 and current fire safety legislation, delivering audit-ready documentation.

Whether you manage a single London office block or a multi-site portfolio across the capital, we deliver consistent, standards-compliant inspections with clear reporting and prioritised remedial recommendations.
