iAUDITOR TEMPLATE — Fire Door Inspection
DE Fire Compliance
==========================================

HOW TO SET THIS UP IN iAUDITOR
--------------------------------
1. Go to app.safetyculture.com → Templates → Create Template
2. Add each section and question below in order
3. Set response types as indicated
4. Add your logo under Template Settings → Branding
5. Save and publish to your mobile app
6. Takes approx 30–45 minutes to build

===========================================
TIPS FOR USING iAUDITOR ON SITE
===========================================

- Download the template to your phone before arriving on site
- Open the inspection and fill in Job Details before you start
- Use the repeatable Door section — tap "Add Item" for each new door
- Work through the checklist quickly using taps — most questions are yes/no or dropdown
- Take photos as you go — camera opens directly inside the app
- If you lose signal, iAuditor saves locally and syncs when back online
- Mark the inspection complete when you're done — this triggers PDF generation
- Send the PDF directly from iAuditor to the client

Free tier: up to 10 inspections/month (enough to start)
Paid tier: ~£24/month for unlimited inspections + advanced reporting
