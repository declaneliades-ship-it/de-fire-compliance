Our Services

Inspection, Compliance & Remediation

End-to-end fire compliance support — from identifying the problem to fixing it. Clear reports, competitive pricing, fast delivery.

Fire Door Inspections

Every fire door assessed against BS 8214 and current legislation. Inspections carried out by qualified carpenters and firestoppers with hands-on trade experience, with all reports produced by an FDIS-qualified professional.

  • Gaps — leaf to frame, threshold
  • Intumescent strips & cold smoke seals
  • Door closer operation & adjustment
  • Hinges, locks & ironmongery
  • Glazing & glazing bead integrity
  • Certification labels & signage
  • Frame condition & fixings
  • Pass/fail per door with photographic evidence
High Demand

Compartmentation Surveys

Comprehensive assessment of fire-resisting barriers, fire stopping, and passive fire protection. We identify breaches in walls, floors, ceilings, and service penetrations that compromise compartment integrity.

  • Fire-resisting walls & partitions
  • Floor & ceiling penetrations
  • Service risers & ducts
  • Fire stopping & cavity barriers
  • Dampers & fire-rated glazing
  • Photographic evidence of all breaches
  • Severity-rated findings
  • Remedial scope & budgetary guidance

Compliance Reports & Support

Audit-ready documentation designed for building managers, compliance officers, and external auditors. We help you understand your obligations and close compliance gaps.

  • Pass/fail assessments per element
  • Severity ratings — critical, high, medium, low
  • Regulatory references (BS 8214, RRO 2005)
  • Prioritised remedial recommendations
  • Budgetary guidance for remedial works
  • Executive summaries for stakeholders
  • Full audit trail documentation

Remedial Works

Identified defects need fixing. We scope, quote, and coordinate remedial works — from fire door repairs and replacements to fire stopping and compartmentation reinstatement. One point of contact from survey to resolution.

  • Fire door repairs & replacements
  • Fire stopping reinstatement
  • Compartmentation remediation
  • Scope defined from inspection findings
  • Quoted separately per project
  • Post-remediation verification available

Our reports are designed for decision-makers. No ambiguity, no jargon — just clear findings, photographic evidence, and prioritised actions.

  • Photographic Evidence Every defect documented with high-resolution photographs and precise descriptions.
  • Pass / Fail Per Element Immediate clarity on which doors and areas are compliant and which require action.
  • Severity Ratings Critical, high, medium, and low — so you can prioritise remedial spend effectively.
