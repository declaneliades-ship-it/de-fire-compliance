TIPS FOR OUTREACH:
- Personalise every email — mention the building name, their organisation, or something specific
- Send 5-10 emails per day (not bulk — individual, targeted)
- Follow up after 3 days, then again after 7 days
- Best times to send: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-9am or 1-2pm
- Use LinkedIn in parallel — connect with facilities managers, housing officers, care home managers
- Track who you've contacted in a simple spreadsheet (name, org, date sent, follow-up date, response)
